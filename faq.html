Frequently Asked Questions

What is Fashora? ▶
Fashora is a personalized styling service that curates fashion choices based on your preferences.
How does this work? ▶
You take a style quiz, and we provide a curated list of fashion items tailored to your style.
Can I update my style preferences later? ▶
Of course! You can change your preferences anytime in your profile settings.
How will you know my size? ▶
We ask for your measurements in the style quiz to ensure the best fit.
Do you style non-working women or style for non work-related occasions such as weddings? ▶
‘Working women’ is our mission for now! We’re venturing into new spaces slowly, so keep an eye out on our social media.
Why should I opt for Fashora instead of hiring a personal stylist? ▶
A personal stylist in the traditional sense would offer you their services at a significant fee over several time consuming consultations. At Fashora, you can have access to the same level of personal attention and shopping services in a very time-conscious,affordable way. All of this can be done from the convenience of your home and in a fun, interesting manner!
Do you have showrooms? ▶
None at the moment, but maybe someday.
How does your AI stylist work? ▶
Our AI analyzes your preferences, body type, and fashion goals to suggest personalized outfits and wardrobe updates.
Is the AI stylist free to use? ▶
We offer both free and premium plans with advanced styling features.
Do I need to upload photos for the AI to work? ▶
Not necessarily. You can answer a style quiz, but uploading images enhances accuracy.
How can I contact support if I have issues? ▶
You can reach out via email or live chat for assistance.
