How It Works

Our stylist reviews your wardrobe, preferences, body type, and shopping habits to identify gaps and craft a personalized plan to enhance or refresh your collection.

Strategic Wardrobe Planning

We tailor your wardrobe to your lifestyle, upcoming events, seasons, and local climate, prioritizing items you need now while identifying nice-to-have additions.

Strategic Budget Planning

A comprehensive breakdown of your budget, highlighting where to save and where to invest.

Shopping done by us

Our stylist shops a collection of pieces tailored to your style, budget, and personal needs.

Closet Detox
